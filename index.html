How to Play Pig Dice

  • Pig Dice is a classic 2-player numbers game.
  • The object of the game is to be the first person to reach 100 points.
  • Each player has a chance to roll the die and either keep your points and roll again, or keep your points and hold, passing play to the next player.
  • If you roll numbers 2-6, they are added to each player's scoreboard, helping you reach that goal of 100 points.
  • HOWEVER, IF YOU ROLL A 1 you lose all of your points and play gets passed to the next player.
